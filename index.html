FAQ

Good questions.

Is QR Master free?

Yes — entirely free, no accounts and no ads.

Where is my scan history stored?

Only in this session, on your device. Refreshing or closing the tab clears it — nothing is sent anywhere.

Which formats are supported?

QR, Data Matrix, Aztec, PDF417, EAN-8/13, UPC-A/E, Code 39/93/128, ITF and more.

Does it work offline?

After your first visit, scanning and generating work without a connection.
